Kodi's Official Page

Who is he?

Kodi is a rescue dog who is about to turn three!

What does he like to do?

Hikes. Lots of hikes. He loves getting into the mountains. A beach day is also looked upon fondly; especially a wide open beach.

What about food?

Human food above everything. Although, he rarely gets any. His favorites include:

  • Beef
  • Chicken
  • Pork
  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Tortilla chips
  • Peas

What about dislikes?

In order:

  1. Loud noises
  2. Sudden movements
  3. The rain
  4. A toy being stolen

PS - Kodi is short for Kodiak
